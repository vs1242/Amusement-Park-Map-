BATMAN™ The Ride

See what it’s like behind the mask on this deeply inaccessible, 50-mph juggernaut as you star in your own GOTHAM adventure in this thrilling BATMAN roller coaster.

Climb aboard the BATMOBILE and prepare to sweep through the city suspended from an overhead track, your feet dangling in the air. With five inversions and enough G-force to plaster you to your seat, this ride is not for the timid.

Following a 10-story lift to the top of the world, brace yourself for a full 360-degree loop right from the start. Then, brace yourself for a fully inverted roll and a second loop about ten seconds later.

Next, you’ll be propelled into pure adrenaline by a double set of wingover loops and corkscrew descents. Watch the city below as you’re swept away just like the criminals of GOTHAM CITY.

Defy gravity as you quadruple it. As you soar above the park, you’ll briefly experience 4 G-force, pressing you to your seat with four-times the amount of typical gravity; all while taking on the ultimate gravity-defying twists and turns.

Minimum height: 54"

Duration: 2 min 15 sec
